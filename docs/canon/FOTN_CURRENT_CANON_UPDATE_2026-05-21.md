# Functions of the Nothing — Current Canon Update

Date: 2026-05-21  
Status: Active Canon Update / Collaborator Sync

## Project Identity

Functions of the Nothing is no longer primarily a card game. The card game was the seed architecture.

Current identity:

**A deterministic pressure-continuity civilization simulator.**

Core formula:

`Pressure → Function → Consequence → Audit → Continuity → Meaning`

Players enter a pressure-governed continuity civilization where actions are observed, attributed, remembered, and allowed to affect long-term meaning.

## Sophia Canon

Sophia is a book.

Sophia must not be represented primarily as a humanoid, goddess, oracle, or AI face.

Canonical representation:

**Sophia’s Guidance — Living Continuity Codex**

Sophia is interpretive guidance, contextual literacy, illumination without control, and memory made readable.

Sophia does not command. Sophia illuminates. The player reads, interprets, acts, and becomes responsible.

Mandatory doctrine:

> You are not following Sophia. You are reading the world.

## Diamond Complex Canon

The Diamond Complex is backend-only.

It must not be treated as a player city, public geography, hub area, visible capital, or inhabitable world space.

The Diamond Complex is invisible continuity orchestration infrastructure.

## Current World Identity

The world is not static geography. The world is layered pressure interpretation.

Current world identity:

**A lived-in continuity civilization.**

Players enter the system, learn through Sophia’s Guidance, choose Paths of Pressure, act inside districts, create social pressure, build reputation, form parties and guilds, perform rituals, and leave legacy.

Player identity doctrine:

> You are not a hero. You are a Function.

Progression doctrine:

> Players do not level up. They become increasingly recorded.

## Economy Doctrine

Value must circulate or return to Nothing.

The economy is pressure circulation, not wealth accumulation.

## Ritual Doctrine

Rituals bind actions to continuity.

Core chain:

`Dedicate → Declare → Seal → Witness → Audit → Legacy`

## Watcher Doctrine

The Watcher does not interfere. The Watcher remembers.

The Watcher is memory, not judgment.
