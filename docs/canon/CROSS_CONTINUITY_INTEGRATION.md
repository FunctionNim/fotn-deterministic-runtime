## Official Project Structure

The project contains two distinct but connected game layers.

### Primary Game

# Functions of the Nothing
## Hermetic Principles of the Lower Vibration

**Role:** Foundational pressure cosmology and pressure-engine architecture.  
**Development Order:** Must come first.

Functions of the Nothing establishes:

- the Functions
- pressure philosophy
- consequence systems
- Momentum doctrine
- continuity mechanics
- backend system architecture
- ontology foundations
- player understanding of survivable pressure

This is the foundational continuity engine of the project.

---

### Secondary Game

# Functions of Sophia
## A Philosophical Sandbox MMO Simulation

**Role:** Experiential continuity exploration layer.  
**Development Order:** Built after foundational stabilization.

Functions of Sophia establishes:

- exploration
- civilization continuity
- Sophia guidance
- environmental continuity
- stories
- citizens
- caravan systems
- relational discovery
- philosophical sandbox progression

This is the player-facing continuity exploration layer built upon the pressure architecture of Functions of the Nothing.

---

## Cross-Continuity Integration Rule

> Functions of Sophia may expand, pressure-test, and clarify foundational ontology.
>
> However:
>
> Functions of the Nothing remains the foundational authority from which all higher continuity systems emerge.
