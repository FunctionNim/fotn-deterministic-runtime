# Continuity Fabrication Doctrine

Status: Canon integration draft.
Issue: #21.

## Core Rule

Playable experiences are constructed continuity environments, not disconnected matches.

The continuity contains operational fabrication systems that prepare, synchronize, audit, receive, and process games.

## Fabrication Purpose

Continuity fabrication exists to:

- construct pressure environments for player training;
- prepare citizen and trainer participation;
- stabilize deterministic replay conditions;
- generate Trial environments;
- preserve returned games for archive processing;
- extract Verdict material from completed experiences;
- support Sophia guidance and Gold observation.

## Continuity Processing Complexes

A Continuity Processing Complex is an operational system used by citizens to create and receive playable experiences.

It is not an ordinary factory. It is a Function-driven continuity fabrication system.

## Experience Lifecycle

1. Construct the pressure environment.
2. Synchronize participants and continuity conditions.
3. Activate audit and replay trace.
4. Execute the experience through deterministic runtime systems.
5. Receive the completed experience back into continuity.
6. Process outcomes into replay, archive, Verdict, Trial, and training material.

## Function Alignment

- Brown constructs the environment and operational structure.
- Green stores returned records and maintains continuity circulation.
- Gold observes, classifies, and interprets traces.
- Stone validates stability and preserves system truth.
- Purple converts completed experiences into reusable material.
- Blue routes participants, states, and replay traces.
- Pink exposes contradictions and creates Verdict questions.
- Sophia translates processed records into player-facing understanding.

## Implementation Rule

Fabrication systems must connect to runtime, replay, synchronization, citizen, Verdict, Trial, Gold, Sophia, archive, and audit systems.

A fabricated experience is valid only if it can be audited, replayed, interpreted, and returned to continuity processing.
