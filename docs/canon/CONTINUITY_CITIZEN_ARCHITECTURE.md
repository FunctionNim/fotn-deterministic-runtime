# Continuity Citizen Architecture

Status: Canon integration draft.
Issue: #21.

## Core Rule

Functions of the Nothing is not primarily a player-versus-player system.

The player enters a living continuity populated by Citizens, Trainers, Witnesses, and Function-aligned continuity participants.

Citizens are not bots, CPUs, or fake players. Citizens are operational participants inside the continuity.

## Purpose

Citizens exist to:

- construct playable pressure environments;
- maintain continuity infrastructure;
- process returned games;
- archive replay records;
- generate Verdict material;
- support Trial environments;
- teach Function pressure through interaction.

## Match Identity

A match is not an abstract disconnected game.

A match is a constructed continuity experience prepared by the system and its citizens.

After completion, the experience returns to the continuity for audit, replay, Verdict, Trial, archive, and processing.

## AI Doctrine

Citizen behavior must prioritize Function expression before pure victory optimization.

A valid Citizen should create recognizable pressure, demonstrate Function philosophy, preserve deterministic legality, and teach consequence through play.

## Implementation Rule

Citizen systems must integrate with runtime, replay, synchronization, Sophia, Gold, Verdict, Trial, and audit systems.

UI expansion should remain downstream of stable continuity architecture.
