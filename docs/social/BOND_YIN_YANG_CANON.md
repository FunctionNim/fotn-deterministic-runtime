# Bond and Yin Yang Canon

## Function Layer

- Black = Nation
- White = Company

These remain raw Function-pressure philosophies.

## Civilization / Social Continuity Layer

- Black beyond Function expression becomes Yin Yang.
- White beyond Function expression becomes Bond.

Pure Black civilization and pure White civilization are too extreme for stable player-facing civilization continuity.

## Bond

Bond represents stabilized relational continuity.

Bond governs:
- guilds,
- covenants,
- persistent alliances,
- long-term trust systems,
- stewardship institutions.

Bond preserves continuity through stable relational persistence.

Bond symbol:
- black on the left,
- white on the right,
- merged into one stable symbol.

## Yin Yang

Yin Yang represents recursive relational motion.

Black and white spin around each other continuously.

Yin Yang governs:
- party systems,
- temporary mission groups,
- adaptive alliances,
- dynamic cooperation under pressure.

Yin Yang preserves continuity through recursive relational contradiction.

Yin Yang symbol:
Traditional Yin Yang structure with continuous rotational motion.

## Final Distinction

Bond = enduring merger.
Yin Yang = continuous conversation.
