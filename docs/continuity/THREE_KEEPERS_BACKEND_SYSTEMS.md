# The Three Keepers

The Three Keepers are backend continuity-operating systems located within the Diamond Complex.

They are not individuals, NPCs, rulers, or player-facing entities.

## ZintosNim — Synchronization Keeper

Responsibilities:
- continuity synchronization,
- timing coherence,
- state alignment,
- pressure harmonization,
- replay-safe continuity agreement.

Core law:

> Reality must remain synchronized enough to survive.

## DyroneNim — Attribution Keeper

Responsibilities:
- audit attribution,
- causality preservation,
- replay traceability,
- consequence lineage,
- observer recording.

Core law:

> Consequence must remain attributable.

## NimF-Gree — Survivability Keeper

Responsibilities:
- survivable evolution,
- adaptive persistence,
- continuity maturity,
- pressure adaptation,
- civilization persistence.

Core law:

> The system must continue becoming without collapsing into irrelevance.

## Combined Role

The Three Keepers collectively govern:

- Synchronization
- Attribution
- Survivability

These are the three requirements of meaningful continuity.
