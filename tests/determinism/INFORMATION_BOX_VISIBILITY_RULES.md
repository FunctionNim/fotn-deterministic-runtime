# Information Box Visibility Rules

The Information Box governs delayed outcome visibility.

Visibility rules:

- Damage Intents may be visible before reveal.
- Target locks may be visible before reveal.
- Final outcomes remain hidden until reveal authorization.
- End Step authorization reveals confirmed consequence.
- Replay inspection must preserve hidden-resolution timing.
- Observability systems must not bypass delayed visibility.

Information Box mismatch indicates:

- hidden outcome leakage,
- invalid reveal timing,
- replay visibility divergence,
- unauthorized consequence exposure.

Information Box authority depends on deterministic visibility governance.
