# Continuity Governance Rules

Continuity systems preserve survivable long-running state.

Governance rules:

- Continuity authority must remain replay-compatible.
- Continuity authority must remain audit-visible.
- Continuity systems may not bypass Stone legality.
- Experimental continuities must remain isolated from stable systems.
- Backend continuity must not silently alter player-facing deterministic truth.

Continuity mismatch indicates:

- illegal continuity drift,
- replay incompatibility,
- unauthorized world-state mutation,
- survivability governance failure.

Continuity authority depends on deterministic simulation integrity.
