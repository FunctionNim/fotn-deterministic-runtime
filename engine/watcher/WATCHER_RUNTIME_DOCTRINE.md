# Watcher Runtime Doctrine

The Watcher reveals deterministic reality without altering it.

Rules:

1. The Watcher may read audit output.
2. The Watcher may read replay frames.
3. The Watcher may read pressure state.
4. The Watcher may read Information Box reveal state.
5. The Watcher must never mutate simulation state.
6. The Watcher must not reveal hidden outcomes before system-authorized visibility.
7. Gold Lens interpretation is read-only.

Purpose:

The Watcher runtime exposes consequence, causality, pressure, and delayed reality to UI and analysis systems while preserving deterministic authority.
