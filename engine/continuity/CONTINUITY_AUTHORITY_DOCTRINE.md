# Continuity Authority Doctrine

Continuity authority preserves survivable system history without overriding deterministic truth.

Rules:

1. Continuity systems classify long-running state, not immediate combat truth.
2. Continuity authority must not bypass Stone constraints.
3. Continuity authority must not bypass replay validation.
4. Continuity maturity must be classified before expansion.
5. Backend continuity may inform world systems without becoming player-facing rules.
6. Every continuity-affecting action must remain auditable.

Maturity classifications:

- Stable
- Experimental
- Restricted
- Dormant
- Mythic
- Core
- PlayerFacing
- BackendOnly

Purpose:

Continuity authority lets long-running systems remain survivable, readable, and synchronized across future MMO, Trial, replay, and world-loop layers.
